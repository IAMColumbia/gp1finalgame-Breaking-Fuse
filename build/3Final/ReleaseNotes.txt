Release Notes version 3 Final

In this version:
 - Instructions displayed in the Intro for the Player
 - The player can Guard using the left/right arrow keys
 - The player can Move using the A and D keys
 - Enemy Wall-Turrets spawn randomly on each tile
 - Coins spawn randomly on full-ground tiles
 - Collecting a coin gives the player +1 to their score
 - Successfully guarding a projectile gives the player +1 to their score
 - A working highscore system
 - UI Elements such as Score and Survival Time
 - Replayability
